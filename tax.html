FAQs

Common Questions

How is this different from Tax-Ready Books?

Tax-Ready Books ensures clean, compliant financials. Business Tax Services adds preparation, secure e-filing, and proactive planning.

Do you work with my current CPA?

Yes. We can coordinate with your CPA, or file via our licensed CPA/EA partners—your choice.

Can you support multi-state returns?

Yes—additional state returns can be included when needed. We’ll scope this during discovery.

Do you serve clients outside Houston?

Absolutely. Engagements are securely handled online for clients nationwide.

What should I prepare for the first call?

Prior-year returns, entity details, access to bookkeeping, and any notices received. We’ll send a concise checklist after you book.
